12 Thy word back next

    Thy word is a lamp unto my feet and a light unto my path. (2x)


    When I feel afraid, think I've lost my way, still You're there right beside me;
    and nothing will I fear as long as You are near,
    please be near me to the end.

    I will not forget Your love for me and yet my heart forever is wandering;
    Jesus be my guide, hold me to Your side, and I will love You to the end.


    Dis Wort isch es Liecht uf mim Wäg, und es lüüchtet mine Füess. (2x)


    Hätti nid dis Wort as mi Troscht i aller Not - i wüssti nid, was i täti!
    S wiist de rächti Wäg, schänkt Erquickig jede Tag - Herr,
    dis Wort isch wunderbar!

    Jedes Wort isch wahr, s'bliibt beschtoh für immerdar; es lehrt mi dini Wäge.
    Meh as Gold und Gäld, als alles i de Wält, liebi dini Wiisige.
